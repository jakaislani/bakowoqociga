8 Things You Didnt Know About Komal Chhabria

➡ Komal Chhabria Biography

Komal Chhabria is a well-trained Indian actress, works in the Hindi film industry. She has been working since a long and made her career prolific. The talented actress took the start of her career through theatre plays and showed her brilliant performances over there. Initially, she appeared in An ODe to MV Wisdom and since then she never looked back and started working with famed directors, such as; Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Vikram Bhatt, and other renowned filmmakers. However, the actress did outstanding work so far and became famous for her contribution to the cinema. Today, we are bringing you some lesser facts to know more about her.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Komal Chhabria

1- Multi-talented Star

Komal Chhabria is a multi-talented star whose skills are not limited to just acting, yet she is an adorable voice artist who worked on a TV channel and RJ (Radio Jockey) as well. Apart from this, she did various acting projects for which she got immense love and appreciation from the audience.

2- Worked with Bollywood Bigwigs

Komal Chhabria worked with Bollywood bigwigs, well-known film directors including Vikram Bhatt on a web series named ‘Hadh’, BBC World web series named ‘Criminal Justice’, ‘Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s ‘Padmavat, and others are notable.

3- Worked in Yashraj Films

In 2018, Komal Chhabria worked with Yashraj Film Production and appeared in the film ‘Sui Dhaga’ which is a drama film, starring Anushka Sharma and Varun Dhawan. While the film was produced by Aditya Chopra and Maneesh Sharma.

4- Method Actor

She was so enthusiastic about her acting profession and that’s the reason she got enrolled in an acting workshop along with her son and got trained as a Method Actor.

5- Non-Smoker

Everyone has different interests and when it comes to smoking, Chhabria never shows her likeness for alcohol and cigarettes she is a non-smoker and she uses non-nicotine cigarettes while playing the role in ‘Suttabaazi’.

6- Fan of Rahul Dev Burman

Being a music fan, the talented lady, Komal Chhabria calls herself a huge fan of Rahul Dev Burman who is a renowned Indian Music Director, known for his music composing styles.

7- Critically Acclaimed Lead Role

The actress also made an outstanding appearance in ‘Adhe Adhure’ in which she portrayed the role of Savitri as a lead actress ad this helped her win numerous awards from theatre experts.

8- Featured in Viral Video on Mother’s Day

Komal Chhabria got featured in TVF’s viral video as a mother which was released in 2017 on the account of Mother’s Day. The video titled ‘Moms First Video Call’ streamed on the most popular platform YouTube.
