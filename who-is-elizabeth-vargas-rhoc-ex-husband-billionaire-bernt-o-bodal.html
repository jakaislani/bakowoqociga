Who Is Elizabeth Vargas RHOC Ex-Husband Billionaire Bernt O. Bodal?

Bernt Bodal is a Norwegian-American businessman, TV personality, and former musician who has a net worth of $200 million dollars.

He is notable for being the former CEO of American Seafoods, one of the largest seafood companies in the US. Bernt is also a partner in the Rock N Roll Fantasy Camp.

Bernt was born on June 14, 1953, in Oslo, Norway. His father worked as a cable car conductor, and his mother was from Lofoten. He has two siblings , Anders Bodal and Håvard Bodal.

Bernt married twice in his life. He married Elizabeth Vargas in 1978 when he was 25 years old.

The duo met one of those days Bodal used to visit his relative’s house in Lofoten and later moved to the United States.

The duo had a son named Thomas Bodal, a daughter named Ellen Bodal, and another son, Alexander Olav Bodal. The couple divorced shortly after the birth of their children.

Following that, he met and began dating TV actress Elizabeth Lyn Vargas. After a while, they both tied a knot with each other. Although, Elizabeth and Bernt do not have a child together.
