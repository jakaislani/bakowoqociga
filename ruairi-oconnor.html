An Irish actor, Ruairi O’Connor who is mainly known for his role as Henry Tudor, Henry VIII, in Starz series titled ‘The Spanish Princess’. Also, he acted in ‘Handsome Devil’ and ‘The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It’.

The charming actor grew up in Howth, Country Dublin, Ireland. He then moved to the countryside along with his family when he was just 14 years old. He completed his bachelor’s degree in acting from The Lir Academy at Trinity College Dublin in 2015.

Career

O’ Connor started off his acting career with a role of Niall in the Lenny Abrahamson film What Richard Did. In 2016, he played a guest role in the RTE2 series titled ‘Can’t Cope, Won’t Cope’ and later, he played a recurring role in the BBC Northern Ireland series titled ‘My Mother and Other Strangers’.

The actor then landed his first major role as Michael Vincent on the television Sky One series named ‘Delicious’ started in 2016 and ended in 2018. In the same year, the actor appeared in the film named ‘Handsome Devil’.

Among others, ‘Teen Spirit’, ‘The Spanish Princess’, and others are popular and worth sharing to watch. However, Ruairi O’ Connor has won millions of hearts with his stand out performances.
