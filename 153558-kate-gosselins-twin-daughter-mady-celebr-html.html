Kate Gosselins Twin Daughter Mady Celebrates School Graduation by Sharing a Sweet Photo with Brot

Kate Gosselin’s twins Mady and Cara graduated high school. The elder twin announced the great news on Instagram, posting a photo of herself with two of her sextuplet brothers.

The 18-year-old twins of Kate Gosselin, Mady and Cara, recently graduated high school from a private school in Pennsylvania. Mady celebrated her milestone with a photo which she posted on her Instagram account.

The graduate dressed up in a white wrap around dress and posed along the hallway of her school with two of her 15-year-old sextuplet brothers, Aaden and Joel. Unfortunately, she did not post any photos with her twin.

OFF TO COLLEGE

The twins’ father, Jon Gosselin’s, attendance during the graduation ceremony was not confirmed, but it is known that his relationship with some of his children is quite unstable.

During the Television Critics Association Winter press tour, Gosselin shared that her twins were good girls, leaving her no worries about their college journey.

“I think that they’re such good girls that I don’t have to keep after them,” the mom of eight said. “They’re self-driven. They have goals and dreams, and they’re just good kids.”

“KATE PLUS DATE”

With the twins about to set foot on a new journey, an upcoming reality show, “Kate Plus Date,” was created as their mom sets foot back into the dating scene, following her 10-year split with Jon. Mady and Cara are hopeful for their mother to find a partner, not wanting her to be alone once college begins.

“As we’re talking about Mady and Cara going off to college which is becoming more and more of a reality, it’s gonna happen, it almost became like a worry for them, too, like ‘Mommy, you’re gonna be all alone soon,'” Gosselin told People.

TYPE A TWINS

Like their mother, Mady and Cara have Type A personalities, being very driven and goal-oriented in life, despite their parents’ divorce. Mady once told People that “the last thing I have time for is a toxic relationship,” perhaps about her relationship with her father.

The twins are very mature, even when it came to their parents’ dramatic divorce, which they claim wasn’t hard for them. They have been doing “better than ever” since the split.

FATHER-DAUGHTER RELATIONSHIP

“He makes it seem like we’re being kept from him, which is insane,” said Mady. She advised her father to “spend more time thinking about why we don’t want to see him” instead of talking to the media about it.
