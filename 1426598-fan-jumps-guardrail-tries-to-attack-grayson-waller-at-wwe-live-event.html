Fan Jumps Guardrail, Tries To Attack Grayson Waller At WWE Live Event

Grayson Waller is a heat magnet.

WWE hosted a live event in Newark, Delaware on Sunday night. Grayson Waller and Austin Theory teamed up against Cameron Grimes & Odyssey Jones.

Prior to the match, Waller mocked some fans, as a heel would typically do. One fan jumped the barricade and tried to go after Waller but security stopped him and ejected him from the arena.

Waller can be seen reacting to the incident in a fan video from the event. He simply struts away from the area, or you could say that he just remembered to Shake It Off.

Results

Full results from the show, courtesy of WrestlingBodyslam.com, are below:

  • Shotzi defeated Bayley
  • Bobby Lashley & Angelo Dawkins defeated The O.C. (Luke Gallows & Karl Anderson)
  • Butch defeated Karrion Kross (w/ Scarlett)
  • Santos Escobar defeated Dragon Lee
  • Viking Rules Match: Kofi Kingston defeated Ivar
  • Austin Theory & Grayson Waller defeated Cameron Grimes & Odyssey Jones
  • WWE Women’s Championship – IYO Sky (c) defeated Charlotte Flair to retain
  • LA Knight & Kevin Owens defeated Jimmy Uso & Solo Sikoa
