(1909-2015) Who Was Sir Nicholas Winton?Sir Nicholas Winton was a 29-year-old stockbroker who in 1939 organized trains out of Prague to secure the safe passage of 669 Jewish children from Czechoslovakia to England at the dawn of World War II. The evacuees, later known as "Winton's Children," knew little about their rescuer until the 1980s, when his work finally came to light.
