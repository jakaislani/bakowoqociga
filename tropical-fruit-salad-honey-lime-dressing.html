    • Tropical Fruit Salad Ingredients

    This salad is a showstopper. Papayas, mangoes, kiwis, pineapple, strawberries, and star fruit make this summer fruit salad impossible to resist. Let’s take a look at what you’ll need:

    For the fruit:

    • papaya – you want ripe but firm papayas so they don’t disintegrate in the salad.
    • mango – look for ripe but firm mangoes.
    • kiwis – love that beautiful pop of green in this salad.
    • strawberries – for sweetness and color.
    • pineapple – fresh is best but you can use canned if you need to.
    • shredded coconut – rounds out the tropical flavors and gives one more texture to enhance the flavor and taste of this salad.
    • star fruit – sliced so each piece is a star shape

    For the fruit salad dressing:

    Just 3 ingredients in this simple dressing!

    • honey – agave nectar can be substituted if desired.
    • fresh lime juice – nothing beats fresh but bottled will work in a pinch.
    • fresh lime zest – really brightens up the dressing and fruit salad.

    How To Make Tropical Fruit Salad

    This easy fruit salad recipe is so quick to make! Let’s take a look at how to make it:

  • Prep the fruit. Rinse, chop, peel, whatever is needed.
  • Add fruit to a large serving bowl. In a large mixing bowl, combine all of the prepped fruit and then set to the side.
  • Prepare dressing. In a second smaller mixing bowl, whisk the honey, lime juice and zest together.
  • Combine. Pour dressing over the fruit and toss to incorporate.
  • Chill. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

    Tropical Fruit Salad

    This Tropical Fruit Salad with Honey Lime Dressing is going to be the star of the show! Featuring a variety of tropical fruit and tossed with a honey lime dressing, there’s a bright burst of flavors in every bite! This easy salad recipe is bound to become a family favorite!

    Course Side Dish Cuisine American Keyword tropical fruit salad Prep Time 15 minutes minutes Total Time 15 minutes minutes Servings 12 servings Calories 125kcal Author Trish – Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 1 papaya peeled, seeded, diced
    • 1 mango peeled, diced
    • 4 kiwis peeled, diced
    • 1 pint strawberries hulled, sliced
    • 1 small pineapple diced
    • ⅓ cup shredded coconut
    • 1 star fruit sliced

    Dressing

    • ¼ cup honey
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
    • 1 tablespoon lime zest

    Instructions

    • Combine fruit in a large bowl.

    • Stir honey, lime juice and zest together in a small bowl.

    • Drizzle dressing over the top of the fresh fruit.

    • Gently toss to combine.

    • Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
