Overall Meaning

David Ingles's song Oasis of Love is about a person who has come to a place of love and peace after a period of aimless wandering. The lyrics describe the singer's journey from a dry and parched land to the oasis of love, where the pastures are green all year round. The singer attributes his transformation to the words of a song from a small Jesus band, which turned his life around.


The Oasis of Love is a place where the hungry are fed, the lonely are given companionship, and those in need are given a home. The singer acknowledges that this place may not be everyone's ideal, but it's all that he needed and it's real. The singer encourages others who are lost in the desert of life to come and drink from the cup of friendship and love that he has found.


The song is a simple but powerful message of hope and transformation. It speaks to people who are lost and alone, offering them a way out of their despair. The message is one of love and acceptance, and the singer is inviting others to join him in the oasis of love. The song is a testament to the transformative power of love and community, and it reminds us that we all need a place of refuge and hope in our lives.
