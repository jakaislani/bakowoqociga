Aidan Hutchinson contract, salary and net worth explored

Aidan Hutchinson, born in Plymouth Michigan, on August 9, 2000, is an American footballer. The 22-year-old plays as a defensive end for the Detroit Lions in the National Football League.

The Lions selected Hutchinson second overall in the 2022 NFL Draft.

Table of Content

Contract

After he was selected by the Lions in the 2022 NFL Draft, he signed a $35 million 4-year deal with the American football giants.

The deal includes a signing bonus of $23,153,372, a guaranteed amount of $35,713,386, a cap hit of $6,493,343, and a dead cap value of $35,713,386.

Salary per year

His annual salary has been pegged at an average sum of $8,928,347.

Net worth

He is currently worth $6 million. Hutchinson attended Divine Child High School in Dearborn, Michigan.

He was chosen to compete in the 2018 U.S. Army All-American Game, recording two sacks. Hutchinson committed to play collegiate football at Michigan when it was time to graduate from high school.

Hutchinson started all 13 games as a true freshman at Michigan, recording 68 tackles and 4.5 sacks. He began the first three games of the 2019 season before suffering a season-ending injury.
