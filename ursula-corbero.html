Úrsula Corberó is a Spanish actress who is known for playing various roles such as Ruth in the Antena 3 series Physics or Chemistry, Margarita de Austria in Isabel, Esther Salinas in The embassy, and Tokyo in the TV series Money Heist. She has also appeared in several music videos such as Cinco de Enero’s El Precio de la Verdad (2008), Heartbreaker (2013), and Mi Querida España (2015). Úrsula realized at a very young age of 6 that she wanted to be an actress and soon started acting in commercials. She is extremely popular on social media where she has more than 7 million followers on Instagram and over 800k followers on Twitter.
