Inside Brooklyn Beckham And Nicola Peltz Married Life

On Saturday, April 9, Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz married in a private ceremony. Brooklyn married Nicola, 27, in a black-tie affair at Peltz’s family home in Palm Beach, Florida. Brooklyn and Nicola’s wedding was a multi-day affair, with guests arriving on April 8 for welcome drinks before the main ceremony on April 9 afternoon. There was also a customary Jewish ritual during the wedding.

Brooklyn Beckham’s renowned parents, David, and Victoria Beckham, as well as siblings Romeo and Cruz and sister Harper, were present at the ceremony to support the 23-year-old on his special day. Nicola’s father, businessman Nelson Peltz, and former model mother, Claudia Heffner, attended the occasion.

The Bates Motel’s star seven siblings were also there, including actor Will Peltz and professional hockey player Brad Peltz. Marc Anthony, Gordon Ramsay, Mel C, Venus and Serena Williams, Eva Longoria, and Jordana Brewster were among those who attended the wedding.

Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz’s relationship

The now-husband and wife were originally connected in October 2019 after being pictured together at a Halloween party. Brooklyn Beckham has just split up with model Hana Cross in August 2019 after dating her for about a year. From 2014 through 2018, the actor was in an on-again, off-again romance with actress Chloe Grace Moretz.

Nicola Peltz, on the other hand, was previously seeing Gigi and Bella Hadid’s brother, Anwar Hadid, before beginning a relationship with Brooklyn. In January 2020, two months after dating, the pair made their love Instagram official by posting images with each other. Brooklyn shared a series of photos of himself and his then-girlfriend-now-wife Nicola posing in front of a bathroom mirror. While Nicola published an intimate mirror photo in which Beckham could be seen naked checking his phone.

Following the announcement of their relationship on Instagram, the pair was observed at various fashion events together, including Paris Fashion Week and the Saint Laurent presentation. Brooklyn proposed to Nicola in July 2020, after they had been dating for less than a year. Beckham recounted asking Peltz to marry him in a gazebo loaded with lights, flowers, champagne, and caviar while appearing on The Late Late Show with James Corden.

“I couldn’t open the bottle of wine, which was quite humiliating. I eventually opened it after 10 minutes, poured her a glass, and went down on my knees. She had no idea what I was up to. I began telling her how much I adore her. It was just around five minutes long. And then I reach back, grab the ring, open it, and ask, ‘Will you marry me?’ Because I want to spend the rest of my life with you. ‘You’re my best buddy,’ I say.”

He went on to claim that Peltz cried for the next five minutes as he waited for a response, which he described as “a little nerve-wracking.” Brooklyn and Nicola had planned to marry before 2022, however, their wedding had to be postponed owing to the COVID-19 outbreak. Beckham announced in an interview with Hello! in November 2021:

“If COVID hadn’t been a thing, we’d have loved to be married by now, and it’s been a little tough.”

Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz played a hilarious game with British Vogue before their wedding, in which the Holidate star stated that she was “terrified for the first dance,” while Beckham admitted that he’d be “more likely to weep on their wedding day.”
